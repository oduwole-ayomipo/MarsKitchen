About

Hundreds of flavours

On September 27, 2012, the first Mars Kitchen restaurant in Lagos, Nigeria, was launched by the company's founder, Oduwole Ayomipo. There are currently seven regions spread across Port Harcourt (Nigeria), Niger, Kenya, Abuja (Nigeria), Nevada, Calabar (Nigeria) and Washington (US). The popular Mars Kitchen Restaurant attracts a wide range of patrons, including families, children, retirees, and business professionals. Our charitable state is ideal for celebrating unusual occasions, arranging a business lunch, or gathering for a delectable supper with loved ones.
We cordially invite you to relax, unwind, and enjoy the magnificent sights and sounds of the ocean as our best chef prepares a delectable dinner for you using only the finest and freshest ingredients. Make the most of your dining experience with us by enjoying our exquisite delicacies!
